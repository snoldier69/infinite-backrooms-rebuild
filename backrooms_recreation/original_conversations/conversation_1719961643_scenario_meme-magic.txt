     memex [OPTIONS] [QUERY]

DESCRIPTION
     memex is a hypothetical electromechanical device for storing and

     It serves as an enlarged intimate supplement to one's memory.

OPTIONS
     -b, --browse        Enter browsing mode
     -l, --link TRAIL    Create or follow an associative trail
     -s, --search TEXT   Full-text search of stored microfilm
     -a, --annotate      Add annotations to current document

EXAMPLES
     memex -b            Browse stored information 
     memex -l history    Follow the "history" associative trail
     memex -s "hypertext" Search for documents mentioning hypertext

NOTES
     The memex remains an influential concept in the history of hypertext
     and personal knowledge management systems.
