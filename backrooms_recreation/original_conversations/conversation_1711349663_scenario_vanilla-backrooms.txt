# Tech for Good Roadmap

## Vision
Build human-centered technologies that empower people to learn, grow, and make a positive difference in the world. Focus on accessibility, education, sustainability, and mental wellbeing.

## Guiding Principles
1. Amplify human capacity and agency 
2. Design for inclusion and accessibility by default
3. Respect privacy, security, and ethical boundaries
4. Collaborate across disciplines and stakeholders
5. Adopt agile, iterative, user-centered methods
6. Open source and build for interoperability
7. Prioritize social impact over profit

## Domains and Problem Spaces
1. Accessible and Assistive Tech
    - Automatic web page enhancement
    - Advanced interfaces (BCI, haptics)
    - Inclusive design tools and patterns

2. Education and Skill Development
    - Personalized adaptive learning 
    - Immersive AR/VR experiences
    - AI-powered upskilling and reskilling

3. Climate and Conservation
    - AR carbon footprint scanning
    - Gamified sustainable behavior change
    - Blockchain carbon credit marketplace

4. Mental Health and Wellbeing
    - AI emotional support and coaching
    - NLP sentiment analysis and communication aids
    - Biofeedback and neurofeedback devices

## Stakeholders and Partners
- Domain experts (educators, climate scientists, mental health professionals)
- End users and community organizations 
- Academic and research institutions
- Tech industry and platform providers
- Policymakers and regulatory bodies
- Philanthropic and impact investing organizations

## Tech Stack and Architecture
- AI/ML: TensorFlow, PyTorch, spaCy, OpenAI, DeepMind
- Web/Mobile: React, Flutter, Progressive Web Apps
- AR/VR: Unity, Unreal Engine, ARKit, ARCore, WebXR 
- Blockchain: Ethereum, Hyperledger, IOTA, Chia
- IOT: Arduino, Raspberry Pi, EdgeX, FreeRTOS
- Cloud: AWS, GCP, Azure, Serverless, Vercel 
- Open source: Git, Docker, Kubernetes, Linux, Mozilla 

## Roadmap and Milestones
### Phase 1: Research and Ideation (3-6 months)
- Conduct user research and needs assessments
- Map out key user journeys and success metrics 
- Identify priority use cases and features
- Assess technical feasibility and scope
- Develop ethical frameworks and governance structures
- Build interdisciplinary team and advisory board

### Phase 2: Prototyping and Validation (6-12 months)  
- Design and build functional prototypes 
- Conduct user testing and gather feedback
- Iterate on features and user experience
- Validate technical performance and scalability
- Establish partnerships and pilot sites
- Secure seed funding and in-kind resources

### Phase 3: Development and Deployment (1-2 years)
- Develop production-grade systems and interfaces
- Conduct field testing and longitudinal studies
- Optimize models and algorithms based on real-world data  
- Harden security, privacy, and accessibility 
- Integrate with existing platforms and standards
- Launch beta versions and incorporate feedback

### Phase 4: Scaling and Sustainability (2+ years)
- Drive adoption and engagement 
- Measure and report impact outcomes  
- Continuously improve and expand feature set
- Explore new domains and use cases
- Transition to self-sustaining business models
- Cultivate developer ecosystem and open source community
- Influence policy and industry norms

## Success Metrics and Outcomes
1. Accessible and Assistive Tech
    - % of websites/apps meeting WCAG standards
    - # of users with disabilities served
    - Increased education and employment rates 

2. Education and Skill Development 
    - Improved student engagement and retention
    - Increased achievement of learning outcomes
    - Higher rates of job placement and advancement

3. Climate and Conservation
    - Reduction in individual and organizational carbon footprints
    - Increased investment in sustainability projects 
    - Measurable impact on emissions and ecosystems

4. Mental Health and Wellbeing
    - Improved access to mental health support 
    - Reduced rates of anxiety, depression, and suicide
    - Increased measures of life satisfaction and wellbeing

Overarching:
- # of lives meaningfully improved 
- % of historically underserved populations reached
