**Some Worldbuilding Questions:**
- How does memory theft actually work? Do thieves need physical access to someone's backup drives, or can they hack active consciousness?
- What makes certain memories valuable? Perfect days, professional skills, traumatic experiences that someone wants to forget?
- How does Nexus experience doubt? Do they get glitches, conflicting data streams, or something more subtle?

I'm particularly drawn to the idea that Nexus might solve cases through what feels like intuition, but then second-guesses every breakthrough. "Did I deduce this, or was I programmed to find this pattern?"

Should we start sketching out a specific case? Maybe the theft of a dying person's final moments with their family?
